Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a question for our team? Here are some of the most popular questions.
If you don't see your query answered below, feel free to contact us here

How do I log out of the platform?

Go to the navigation bar, and click "Log Out" which is positioned on the most right side.

How do I report a problem with the platform?

You can contact us here here.

How do I send a message to a friend?

You can go to your Socials on the navigation bar, and view your friends list. You can proceed to chat with the person by clicking on the 'chat' button.

How do I share a resource to everyone?

Under the "Resources" section, you can click on "Knowledge Repository", and click on a "create a post" button to share.
